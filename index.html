Javascript Quiz

Let's get started!

This will quiz you on your basic Javascript knowledge. There are 5 multiple choice questions. When you select the start button, you will be prompted with your first question. You have 1 minute to answer the 5 questions. See how fast you can answer all 5 questions correctly. Your score will tell you how many seconds you had leftover. The higher the score, the quicker you were able to correctly answer the questions! Good luck!
